Welcome to my firt hand made game :D.

Hello, this is my firt game made with manual coding. Its programmed in the programming langiage Haxe/OpenFL. This is a very simple graphics game to play a kind of tennis with a bot. Controls:

  • Space - to start
  • Arrow up - move your platform up
  • Arrow down - move your platform down
